**vissE.Cloud** is a web application that identifies and visualises higher-order molecular phenotypes from gene-set enrichment analysis, making functional interpretation accessible without writing code. It has served **70,000+ analyses across 42 countries**.

- 📄 *Nucleic Acids Research* (2023) — webserver issue
